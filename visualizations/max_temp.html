Latitude vs Max Temperature

The Latitude vs Max Temperature plot shows the latitude for each city vs the maximum temperature seen that day. Temperatures are warmest closer to the Equator. The Northern Hemisphere data (latitude > 0 deg) shows a broader range of temperatures than the Southern Hemisphere data (latitude < 0 deg). This could be due to the tilt of the earth's axis relative to the sun or that there aren't enough observations in the Southern Hemisphere to collect the full range of temperatures because there is less habitable landmass in the Southern Hemisphere.
